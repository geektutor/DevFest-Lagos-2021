12:55 pm - 1:35 pmBuilding Observable Cloud Native Applicationsby Prosper Opara - Software Engineer - Deimos Cloud

"The cloud-native space is maturing and the market demand for building scalable distributed systems for the billion users has given rise to a high influx of developers and even software development teams seeking to transition and learn more about cloud-native development. More companies are seeking to modernize their systems into cloud-native applications to begin to enjoy the good parts of the cloud-native architecture.
An ancient adage advises that; ""Anything worth doing, is worth doing well"". It's one thing to build cloud-native applications and another to build observable systems that are reliable, meet business and technical requirements and avoid a lot of the pitfalls and pains of managing distributed systems. in this talk I'll be sharing with the audience the basics of observability, battle-tested observability tools, observability best practices for cloud-native applications like; application health checks, logging, distributed tracing, their importance, and why they are key when building for the cloud.
This talk draws from experience building cloud-native applications at Deimos Cloud (A leading cloud-native application development company in Africa) and is intended for software developers, and SRE of any skill level."
